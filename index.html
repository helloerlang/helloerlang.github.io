Episode #3: Nova Framework

In this compelling podcast episode, we dive deep into the realm of Erlang/OTP and shine a spotlight on Nova, a revolutionary web framework designed to simplify the development of fault-tolerant and distributed web applications. Joining us are two trailblazing experts and the brilliant minds behind Nova: Daniel Widgren, the Domain Architect at Kivra, and Niclas Axelsson, a Senior Software Engineer at Telia.
